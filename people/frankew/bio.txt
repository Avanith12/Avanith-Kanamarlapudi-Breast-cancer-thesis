Loraine is a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Boston. Before joining the Machine Psychology research team, Loraine worked as a research assistant for the chair of Information Systems Research during her Master's degree in Economics at the University of Freiburg, Germany. She holds a BS in technical Business Administration from University of Stuttgart, where she was employed by the IT department of Mercedes-Benz. Loraine studied Medical Sciences for one year at University of Strasbourg in France and is highly motivated to apply data science and machine learning methods on medical topics.
